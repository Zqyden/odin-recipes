HaiChicken Rice


Description

The Hainanese chicken rice is a well-known Southeast Asian dish featuring poached chicken served with fragrant rice cooked in chicken stock, plus a light soy dressing and chili-ginger sauce.

Its appeal comes from clean, rich flavors and the balance between tender chicken and aromatic rice.


Ingredients

  • Whole chicken (poached)
  • Chicken Stock (from the poaching liquid)
  • Jasmine rice
  • Ginger
  • Pandan leaves
  • Salt, light soy saunce, seasome oil
  • Chili, lime, and sometimes sugar (for the dipping sauce)
  • Cucumber and spring onions (for garnish)

Step-by-Step

  1. Salt, rinse, and dry the chicken, then stuff it with ginger and spring onions.
  2. Poach the chicken gently in water with ginger and pandan until cooked through.
  3. Transfer the chicken to an ice bath to stop cooking and firm the skin.
  4. Fry ginger and garlic in oil, then coat rinsed jasmine rice in the mixture.
  5. Cook the rice with measured chicken stock and pandan until fluffy.
  6. Prepare the ginger-garlic oil, chili sauce, and light soy mixture.
  7. Dry and brush the chicken with sesame oil, then chop into serving pieces.
  8. Serve the chicken with the fragrant rice, cucumber, spring onions, sauces, and a side of clear broth.
